I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at [Florida Atlantic University](http://eecs.fau.edu/). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from [Washington University in St. Louis](https://cse.washu.edu/) in May 2025, advised by Dr. [Raj Jain](https://www.cse.wustl.edu/~jain/). My research spans quantum computing, quantum optimization, networking, security, and machine learning. My current work focuses on scalable quantum systems, particularly distributed quantum computing and quantum networking, as well as cross-domain quantum applications in areas such as genomics, biochemistry, and power systems. I have also worked on network performance and decentralization, with an emphasis on resource management, routing, and the integration of blockchain and artificial intelligence. Prior to this, I worked as a senior software engineer at Tencent, Baidu, and DJI from 2011 to 2017. I received my master’s degree in computer engineering from Waseda University, Tokyo, Japan, in 2019.
